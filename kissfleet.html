Client Centered Development

Although this is the latest industry buzzword, at KISS we have been training our driving instructors to teach this way for over ten years. It isn’t difficult to do, it just requires us to ask questions and not tell drivers what to do. With the right training aids (which we supply) and a little practice you can dramatically generate more business for yourself.
Our Fleet Driver Training Course will identify and focus on your needs and our Fleet Trainers will deliver as much one to one as you need. Some of the classroom training needed will be delivered online so you can attend from the comfort of your home. Some will take place in groups to help you develop your skills.
We do ask you to do some preparation work however we do supply the training aides required to make this an easy process e.g. write a brief report. If our Trainers identify any additional training needs please take advantage of the extra help and guidance offered.

Course Options

You can join our rolling Fleet Driver Trainer Course at any time and the only restriction is that you must complete all five units within one year of passing the Theory Test. This allows for as much training as you need and ensures you will qualify and be able to generate income as a Fleet driver trainer.

Course Completion

Once you have completed the five units you can apply to the DSA enter the Fleet Register of trainers. More importantly, to be able to sell your services into businesses you need to be confident in your knowledge and skills required to deliver the results an organisation needs.
